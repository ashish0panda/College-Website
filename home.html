ABOUT US

Hearty welcome to RKCIT which has already set a glorious tradition of success. We are committed to make your learning experience exciting, stimulating and rewarding. Without doubt, RKCIT is the FIRST CHOICE in this part of Odisha. We believe in creating a perfect learning environment by encouraging innovative, affordable, quality teaching and utilizing technology to enable our students to realize their dreams and aspirations. We have already earned a great reputation for high quality teaching in a learner-friendly, caring, supportive, residence based setting which is conducive to learning. We nurture talents through continual testing, evolution and counseling to maximize their potential to get success. The talented faculty at RKCIT, many of whom are outstanding retired professors are our greatest assets.
